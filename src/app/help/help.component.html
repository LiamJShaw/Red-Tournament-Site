Audio Issues

  • Ensure your microphone is connected and recognized by the system.
  • Go to Discord settings (the gear icon) -> "Voice & Video" -> "Input Device".
  • Check if the right microphone is selected. If "Default" isn’t working, try selecting your microphone manually.
  • Make sure your microphone isn’t muted in Discord or through any physical mute button on the device.
  • Check the microphone volume level in Discord settings and make sure it’s not set too low.
  • If these steps don’t work, restart Discord or your device and try again.

  • Ensure your headphone/speaker is connected and recognized by the system.
  • Go to Discord settings (the gear icon) -> "Voice & Video" -> "Output Device".
  • Check if the right output device (headphones or speakers) is selected. If "Default" isn’t working, manually select the device you're using.
  • Check the volume mixer in your operating system (Windows or Mac) to make sure Discord isn’t muted.
  • Ensure your headphones/speakers are not muted via a physical volume control on the device.
  • If these steps don’t work, restart Discord or your device and try again.
