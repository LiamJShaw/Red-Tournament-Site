Tournament Info

Format

This year, we’ll be using a Swiss-style bracket. In this format, everyone will play for the first 4 or 5 rounds. After each round, players gain points for winning. In subsequent rounds, you’ll race against opponents with similar point values, making it a self-balancing system.

After the Swiss rounds, the top 9 players with the highest points will advance to the semi-finals, and then to the finals.

Seeding

Seeding will be done based on WHAT.

Participants will be placed over 3 pots in equal amounts.
